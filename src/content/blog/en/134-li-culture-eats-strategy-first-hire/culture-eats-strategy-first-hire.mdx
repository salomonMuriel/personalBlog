Culture eats strategy for breakfast 🥞. Building a team (with good culture) is brutally hard.

This week we made our first hire at Ignia — and it's genuinely scary.

1. What culture do we want to create? Right now with my co-founder you can "feel" the culture — but we haven't written it down.
2. How do you communicate it? Writing it down isn't enough — you have to demonstrate it.
3. How do you sustain it? Culture needs "reminders" — specific actions and rituals that keep it feeling alive.

I'm no expert, but I know how important it is. [Pablo Armida](https://linkedin.com/in/pabloarmida), [Fernando Sucre](https://linkedin.com/in/fernandosucre), and [Ricardo Buitrago](https://linkedin.com/in/ricardo-buitrago) taught me this firsthand at R5.

Honest question: what do you think are the best ways to build culture in early-stage teams?
