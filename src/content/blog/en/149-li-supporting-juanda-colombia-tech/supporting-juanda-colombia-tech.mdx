A few weeks ago [Estefania Grajales Rojas](https://www.linkedin.com/in/estefania-grajales) invited me to [Juan David](https://www.linkedin.com/in/juandavidaristi)'s house — without a lot of detail about why.

I had met him while researching the education world for Ignia — where he told me his story of entrepreneurship and deep love for education.

The reason was exciting: dreaming of a country we'd actually want to stay in. Where you can build. Where you can grow.

A dinner with about 10 other people where, together, we believed in that dream.

I decided to support JuanDa in achieving that goal through technology. How awesome to team up with someone who's so passionate about building the country 🔥.

I believe the future of the country is in good hands with people like him — people who love to create, who are practical, honest — and who go for it.

People who, above all, actually do something.
