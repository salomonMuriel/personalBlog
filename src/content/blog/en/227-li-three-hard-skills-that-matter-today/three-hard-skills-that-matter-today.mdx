I'm betting on 3 "hard" skills that are truly important right now:

1. **Design Thinking** — You have to understand users and ecosystems before you start building.

2. **Agile Business** — The startup mindset applies to everything, not just tech. Test fast, iterate fast, discard fast.

3. **Technology** — Every field benefits from automating processes and leveraging modern tech (AI, yes, but there's so much more).

And on the soft skills side, I'd add adaptive leadership + a growth mindset.

I believe that combo makes a winning professional in any field — corporate, entrepreneurship, research and academia... whatever you're doing.

What skills would you add? Or is there one that shouldn't be on the list?
