This Thursday and every Thursday we have Ignia Coworking Thursdays ⬇️

We get together to cowork at inspiring spots around the city — to hang out, meet new people, and create 🚀

100% free, open entry, and 10% off the menu at whatever venue we're at 💸. RSVP: [https://luma.com/2it8rurj](https://luma.com/2it8rurj)

We're a community of driven people 🔥 just like you. Whether you're an entrepreneur, freelancer, at a startup, a company, or a foundation. Age or sector don't matter — just the hustle. We'll see you there!

Special guest: [Nicolás Varón](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolasvaronrodriguez), serial entrepreneur, co-founder of the [Asociación de Emprendedores](https://www.linkedin.com/company/asociaciondeemprendedores/), marketing and growth expert, and founder of [Clevoo | Clever Growth](https://www.linkedin.com/company/clevoo-growth/).
