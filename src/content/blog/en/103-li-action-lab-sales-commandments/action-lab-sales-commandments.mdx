Quote of the week — Better intense with money than discreet and broke.

Yesterday we had the fourth session of the Action Lab — Sales with [Santiago Cortes Calle](https://linkedin.com/in/santiagocortescalle) and [Laura Sánchez M.](https://linkedin.com/in/laura-sanchez-m). (Way too good 🔥 )

Santiago should do a Sales Stand-Up Comedy show, and Laura should write a book. Pure gold. They left us with 4 commandments:

1. The gold is in the follow-up. Never stop making contact!
2. Never too much. Not contact, not marketing, not anything. Be everywhere.
3. Not every client wants to hear the same thing. Speak in the other person's language, to their pain points.
4. Not everyone qualifies to be your client. Immediately disqualify those who don't fit your niche and price point.
