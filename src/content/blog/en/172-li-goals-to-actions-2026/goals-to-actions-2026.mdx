Following [Liliana Rodriguez Vega](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lilianarodriguezv)'s advice, I'm shifting from "goals to achieve in 2026" to "actions to take in 2026":

1️⃣ Help Ignia grow 3X in annual revenue, 4X in margin, and 5X in total impact 👉 Do 2 sales actions every day (prospect, reach out, follow up, ask for referrals). Not counting ads or social content.

2️⃣ Read AT LEAST 10 books 👉 Read 50 pages per week.

3️⃣ Run the Bogotá Half Marathon again and beat my time 👉 Run at least twice a week (Sunday + 1 other day).

4️⃣ Meet at least 100 new people 👉 Schedule 1 LinkedIn coffee chat + 1 person from my in-person events per week.

5️⃣ Tell stories at 10 events/podcasts 👉 Post on LinkedIn at least 5 times a week (and let those posts bring the speaking invites!) - PS [Santiago Grisales](https://www.linkedin.com/in/santiago-grisales-bohórquez) thx for the first invite of the year 🔥

Both Atomic Habits and Feel-Good Productivity stress the importance of short-term, measurable progress — let's see if it works!

To track them I decided to use Loop (I'm on Android). Free, no ads, no limits, options for everything. Link: [https://lnkd.in/ewBZtThT](https://lnkd.in/ewBZtThT)

PS - I haven't had a deep conversation with anyone new yet this year. Who wants to grab coffee next week?
