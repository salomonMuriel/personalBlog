If you're in Bogotá, our Coworking Thursdays are back 👇

A space to meet more people with fire for CREATING things of all kinds. And to get your brain moving.

We get founders, foundation builders, project starters, artists, impact makers, movement leaders, startup people, community builders. And people who want to become any of those someday.

In other words: people with fire 🔥

It's simple — we're there from 9am to 5-6pm every Thursday. Free entry (but pre-registration required). We pick inspiring coffee shops around the city.

Come whenever you want, leave whenever you want. Those of us still there at noon have lunch together.

At the end of the afternoon there's a talk or workshop with different guests each week.

Normally 30 to 60 people show up throughout the day. Every single week 🚀

▶️ The first one of the year is Thursday January 15th at La Cesta on Calle 81 (one week from today). The afternoon workshop will be "Tech for Non-Techies" — how to use AI for everyday life and work (given by me, a slightly chaotic talk ⚡)
