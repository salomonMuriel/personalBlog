I'm thrilled to share that today [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/) is officially born!

Together with [Camilo Bonilla](https://linkedin.com/in/camilobonilla), we're taking on the challenge of hacking Higher Education — in Colombia and the world.

Thank you, thank you, thank you to the more than 100 people who gave me their time, energy, warmth, and advice to understand this world, iterate, and shape the idea. And to Cami for bringing so much, connecting minds, and believing in building this together.

Our first program, the Action Lab, has a launch event on June 14 at 4PM at the Irish Pub in Quinta Camacho, Bogotá. The event flyer and registration link are in another post!
