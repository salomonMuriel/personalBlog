They told me it couldn't be done ❌

They told me you couldn't build an educational program in less than 6 months.

They told me that getting teachers, finding venues, planning content, building the platform, and selling it required a team of at least 4 full-time people.

They told me you couldn't sell even 20 spots at $1,200 USD in 2 months. At least 6 months of sales, MINIMUM.

They told me it wasn't sellable — that everyone looks for a quality certification, that without a brand you can't sell anything in education.

Yesterday the Action Lab kicked off. We got 33 driven people 🔥 for the first cohort.

We did it 🚀 . And we'll do more.

[Camilo Bonilla](https://www.linkedin.com/in/camilobonilla), me, the instructors, and everyone who supported us (including a few believers who said YES WE CAN) are going all in to reimagine higher education. It can be done. Who's joining us?
