Speaking of intentional design, and then design completely wasted. How on earth do you build the Ágora (an incredible project), connect it to Corferias through a tunnel-bridge (brilliant idea)... and then not let anyone use it? 😡

I went to GoFest on Wednesday and Friday (fantastic event). Yesterday had lunch with the [Educación Estrella®](https://www.linkedin.com/company/educacion-estrella/) team (super inspiring, thanks [Luis  Cedeño Hernández ](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luiscedeno1), [Wei-maa Hung](https://www.linkedin.com/in/wei-maa-hung), [Santiago Garcés Marulanda](https://www.linkedin.com/in/santiago-garces-m) for the conversation and the meal).

It's raining. There's a tunnel-bridge. I head straight to the parking garage. The tunnel HAS AN ELEVATOR THAT GOES TO THE PARKING GARAGE. I walk through the tunnel, reach the elevator, and a security guard (very politely) tells me I can't use it. And I can't use the tunnel either, please turn around (and go get soaked crossing the street).
