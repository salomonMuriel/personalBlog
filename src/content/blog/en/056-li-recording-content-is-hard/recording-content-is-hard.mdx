I feel completely out of my element 🐕 every time I have to record myself for social media.

Overwhelmed by the complexity: Which angle is best, the script with hook+body+CTA, conversion, engagement and interaction, the right tone, every platform with its own content quirks, 20 takes for each line...

Mad respect to the people who make it look easy: [Pedro Mejia](https://linkedin.com/in/pedromejiar), [Sara Samaniego](https://linkedin.com/in/sara-samaniego-06095a43), [Nicolas Rojas Nino](https://linkedin.com/in/nicolasimagine), [Valentina Ponce de León](https://linkedin.com/in/vpdl) and others.

But you've got to build that muscle: it was more uncomfortable 3 months ago than it is today, and in another 3 it'll be better. Same as everything in entrepreneurship — resilience.

Like when I learned to code, or to understand business models, or the world of VC investing, or building valuable networks...

I think this post is just self-therapy. Time to get those Instagram videos out next week 🎥 and if you have tips, hit me up in DMs or the comments...
