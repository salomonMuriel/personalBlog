Ethos-Pathos-Logos. Credibility 🏆, Emotion ❤️, Logic 🧠. How to tell stories from every human angle, summarized in 3 words.

Yesterday at the Action Lab session we had the one and only [Pedro Mejia](https://linkedin.com/in/pedromejiar) live in the room talking personal branding. Absolute legend 🔥

It's been incredible watching these 3 concepts come to life through the Action Lab.

With [Jose Duarte](https://linkedin.com/in/joseduarteq) and [Luis Felipe Barrientos Moreno](https://linkedin.com/in/luis-felipe-barrientos-moreno) I understood how to apply them to our projects — but now I'm finally getting how to apply them to myself.

Speak from what you've done, tell stories from lived experience, show real data. Ethos-Pathos-Logos.

We have to tell stories that inspire — so our ideas spread.

What's your ethos? What's your pathos? What's your logos?
