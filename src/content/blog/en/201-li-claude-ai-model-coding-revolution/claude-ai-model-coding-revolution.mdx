Honestly, I don't think I'll ever have to write code by hand again. Not exaggerating — I'm one-shotting things at equal quality (or dare I say better) than what I would've done manually over weeks.

Different level.

Crazy times. I don't know what future is coming for everyone (and for my kids especially...) but it's going to look very different from today.
