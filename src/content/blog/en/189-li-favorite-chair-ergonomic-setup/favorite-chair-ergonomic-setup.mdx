My second favorite work gadget — my chair 🪑

I spent the pandemic "locked up" at my family's farm near Apulo (pure luck — that's where the lockdowns caught me). When I came back to Bogotá and realized remote work was going to be more or less permanent, I invested in a home office setup.

That's when the monitors and keyboard arrived — but most importantly, the chair. I love it ❤️

It's from [BOUND Mobiliario](https://www.linkedin.com/company/bound-mobiliario/) — the best option in the category by a wide margin, in my opinion.

I spend about 20 hours a week sitting in it (back in my heavy coding and gaming days it was more like 60-70 🫣)

It's perfect — high and low back support, super comfortable where it counts most (the rear end haha), fully adjustable (height, headrest, recline, armrests, lumbar support).

Five years in and still like new (with my kids jumping on it regularly).

What chair do you have? I've also thought about switching to a standing desk but haven't pulled the trigger. Is it worth it?
