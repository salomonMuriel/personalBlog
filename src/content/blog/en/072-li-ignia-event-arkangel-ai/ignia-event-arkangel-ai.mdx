Thursday we had an incredible event 🔥 — a digital foot health prototype (wtf?) + a panel with [Jose Zea](https://linkedin.com/in/josezea) ([Arkangel AI](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arkangel-ai/)).

60+ in-person attendees, tons of energy and connections.

So great to be able to learn from such an inspiring story as Jose's — a clear example of building from personal experiences and passion.

And the AI prototyping workshop was... different 🦶🏼. We built a digital prototype from an idea the audience came up with: foot health using barefoot/minimalist shoes.

And the ability to collaborate with people and communities like [Miguel Vanegas Torres](https://linkedin.com/in/miguelvanegas) + [Capítulo Empreandinos](https://www.linkedin.com/showcase/capitulo-empreandinos/) to create powerful spaces. We pulled it off in record time — from planning to execution in 1 week ⚡

At [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/) we firmly believe in learning from those who've already done it + learning by doing.

Help us think through the next event 🔝. Who would you want to learn from? What would you want to workshop live?
