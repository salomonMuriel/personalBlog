One beer with [Luis Felipe Barrientos Moreno](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luis-felipe-barrientos-moreno) opened my eyes to the power of storytelling.

Not presentations. Not just talking.

Telling a well-crafted story.

That beer led to my first properly prepared story. Night and day compared to "making a presentation."

I want to tell stories many times this year — about what I've lived and what's coming.

[Santiago Grisales](https://www.linkedin.com/in/santiago-grisales-bohórquez) invited me to tell the first one of the year on his podcast. He also gave me a book 📖. Good coffee, good conversation.

Coming soon on his podcast Remando en Arequipe — a story about the failures, wins, and insights from innovating as an entrepreneur.

Thanks Santi for the invite and for helping me take the first step toward my goal!

Bonus: running into [Alejandro Uribe Lopez](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dauribel) from my DS4A days. We spent an hour talking about Claude Code, digital transformation, and adopting new technologies. Ticking the box on meeting interesting people 🔥

What story do you want to tell that you haven't told yet? Let's find the space — over coffee, at the Coworking Thursdays.
