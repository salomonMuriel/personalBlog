Tomorrow and every Thursday we have [El Parche Remoto](https://www.linkedin.com/company/parche-remoto/) in Bogotá, and this week with [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/) we have an incredible guest 🧨 : [Juan Sebastián  Benítez Bustamante](https://linkedin.com/in/juansebastianbenitezb), serial social entrepreneur.

With [Paguro](https://www.linkedin.com/company/paguroestudio/) they turn construction waste into gorgeous furniture and décor.

With [Local Districts](https://www.linkedin.com/company/distritos-locales/) he works to connect communities and make them more sustainable, neighborhood by neighborhood. The creators of [Distrito CH](https://www.linkedin.com/company/distrito-ch/)!

We're at El Parche Remoto at [Colo Coffee Roasters](https://www.linkedin.com/company/colo-coffee-roasters/) in Usaquén from 9AM to 5PM, co-working together. And [Juan Sebastián  Benítez Bustamante](https://linkedin.com/in/juansebastianbenitezb) will be sharing his story from 3 to 4 🔥
