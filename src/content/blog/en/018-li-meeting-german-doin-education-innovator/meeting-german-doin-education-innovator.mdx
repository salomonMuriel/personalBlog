Last week I had the fortune of spending several days with [German Doin Campos](https://linkedin.com/in/germandoin), putting my learnings (minimal by comparison) from ~90 conversations over the past months up against his 15+ years of experience creating innovative educational proposals.

Incredibly inspiring conversations — learning about his journey from launching "La Educación Prohibida," to founding "Proyecto C" in Buenos Aires, to advising dozens of schools and educational initiatives. Insights on the role of higher education, the challenges in diversity-focused projects, and how to simultaneously develop feeling ❤️ + thinking 🧠 + doing 🛠️...

We also shared spaces with other leaders and actors in education, digging into what the current educational system actually needs. Thank you to [Gimnasio Moderno](https://www.linkedin.com/school/gimnasio-moderno/) for providing a space for reflection and discussion!

All in all — incredible! Getting closer every day to creating something big in Education, alongside people like Germán 🇦🇷
