✅ I built a group challenge tracker — it's right here: [https://tracker.ignia.lat/](https://tracker.ignia.lat/)

At Ignia we got a challenge — 100 days of sales activities.

But we all have challenges every day. Building habits requires consistency.

Working out (which I always fail at 😥 ), reading, time for hobbies, an afternoon with the kids, reviewing company KPIs, meditating, making the daily social media post...

And everything works better in community — accountability is key to actually building those habits.

With this tracker you can — for free — create challenges that repeat X days a week, for Y days in a row. Ignia's sales challenge is 100 consecutive weekdays, for example (you're welcome to join!)

Let me know if it works for you. It's kind of amazing that we can build little tools like this now.

Anything you'd want me to add? What challenge are you going to set (and actually stick to)?
