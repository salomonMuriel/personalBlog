Yesterday we live-built dozens of product prototypes... in 1 hour ⚡ I'm leaving the workshop guide with all the pre-made prompts in case anyone wants to try it at home ⬇️

I was at [Pm Beers-Product Club](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pmbeers/) running a Fast Prototyping with AI workshop 🤖

Cool stuff:

- Packed house. Hache de Hamburguesas on 119th. Fries, Beer 🍻, great energy to get the brain firing.
- Community crossover. Fellows/members from [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/), [LAB10](https://www.linkedin.com/company/lab10ai/), [Pm Beers-Product Club](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pmbeers/) and [Tribu IA](https://www.linkedin.com/company/tribu-ia/) showed up. Amazing 🔥
- Great vibe. We used examples from the audience (WTF — a digital prototype for bulletproof glass windows), there were a couple of screw-ups + technical failures, good conversation and networking.

We used:
1. Gemini Deep Research for user research and identifying pain points.
2. Regular Gemini to refine and surface possible solutions.
3. Fillout to create a feedback form on the spot with AI.
4. Mocha+Windsurf for the prototype and final details.

Thanks [Alvaro Pinzon](https://linkedin.com/in/alvaroandrespincor) and the PM Beers community for the invite!
