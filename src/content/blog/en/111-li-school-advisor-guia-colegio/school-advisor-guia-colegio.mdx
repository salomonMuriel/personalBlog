School selection is so hard... but I finally found a way 🥳  (the potential schools are in the photos)

From my previous post I got a thousand school recommendations... and one tip about a platform for finding them (GuiaColegio).

I checked it out — cool platform (basically the master database of schools in Bogotá, with everything you'd want to know).

But even better — they offer a personalized advisory service to help you understand EVERYTHING about the process. One-on-one, live, online.

And not just that. They walk you through what kind of kids you have, what kind of family you are, what values are important or non-negotiable... AND YOU WALK AWAY WITH A SHORTLIST OF SCHOOLS TO VISIT 🤯

Sounds impossible — going from 1,000 schools in Bogotá to 7 that fit our values, location, and kids perfectly.

Plus — actually understanding the costs. She broke down the annual costs, which schools are worth paying a bond for (and the ROI on each one), explained what all the certifications mean (I had no clue before)... EVERYTHING.
