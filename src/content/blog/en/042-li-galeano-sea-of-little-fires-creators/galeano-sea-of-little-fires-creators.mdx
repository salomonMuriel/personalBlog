There's something so powerful about people with creative drive and an inner fire.

Eduardo Galeano wrote "A Sea of Little Fires." As he beautifully puts it:

> "Some fires (...) neither light nor burn;
> but others, others, burn life with such desire
> that you can't look at them without blinking,
> and whoever gets close, catches fire"

The world needs "people of wild fire who fill the air with sparks." Creators who desperately want to make their ideas real.

The [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/) Action Lab wants to bring together a group of intense fires. And let those fires grow and shine.

What kind of fire are you?
