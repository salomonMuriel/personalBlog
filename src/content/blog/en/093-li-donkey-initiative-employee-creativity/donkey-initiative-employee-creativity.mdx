Yesterday in a WhatsApp group someone posted the old saying 'nothing worse than a donkey with initiative' 🫏

Hard disagree ❌  Nothing worse than someone without the motivation and creativity to take initiative and create things.

If you want to hire robots, you can already do that with AI. If you're going to hire people, you have to give them space to create.
