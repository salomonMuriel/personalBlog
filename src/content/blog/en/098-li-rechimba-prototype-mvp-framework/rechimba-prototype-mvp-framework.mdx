Instead of building MVPs, build RECHIMBA prototypes 😎

Full credit to [Camilo Bonilla](https://www.linkedin.com/in/camilobonilla). I love this idea — a real Colombian spin on the MVP. A RECHIMBA prototype is:

**RE**alizable — Something you can actually build easily, with your current team.
**CHI**quito (Small) — With the fewest features and complexity possible. Focus on the essence.
**M**easurable — Clear KPIs so you know if it worked.
**BA**rato (Cheap) — Using the fewest resources possible in every sense. Time, money, people.

At Ignia we're building 34 RECHIMBA projects with our fellows 🔥 — all kinds: foundations, consultancies, tech projects, corporate spinoffs. RECHIMBA applies to any project.

What do you think? Is anything missing from RECHIMBA?
