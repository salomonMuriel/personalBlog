The era of digital building with AI is for every kind of project — like, for example... a knitting club.

My mom wants to start her knitting club 🧶. How can AI help make it real?

They recently launched [Mocha](https://www.linkedin.com/company/getmocha/), a new "Lovable-style" tool for building digital experiences. I used my mom's idea to build a first version of her website with everything she had in mind. It took me.... 10 minutes.

Check it out 👀 — it's incredible. In just 3 prompts it produced a beautiful page, with killer copy, a waitlist to build hype, a separate Virtual Store tab, a connected database... all without writing a single line of code 🤯. Imagine what you could build in a full day of work. Or in a month.
