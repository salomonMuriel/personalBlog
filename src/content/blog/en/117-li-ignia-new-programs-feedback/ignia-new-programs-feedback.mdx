Give me your opinion please! We want to launch these programs at Ignia 🔥 This is the first reality check for these ideas. What do you think, or what else do you think would be worth doing?

We're going to pick 2-3 to start with.

1️⃣ Sales at Full Throttle — Structuring sales funnels with all the AI, automation, and social-selling know-how — the latest playbooks to close 2x-3x.

2️⃣ Augmented Product Management — How to go from being an average PM to a PM on steroids, seeing how new tools transform team rituals and make it easier to interact with the technical side.

3️⃣ Claude Code for Advanced Devs — Using agentic tools to produce 20X what you used to, teaching your team to do the same, and maintaining quality along the way.

4️⃣ Tech for Non-Techies — How to leverage AI & Tech: automations with n8n, AI with superpowers (not just ChatGPT), productivity tools like Notion — all without needing a technical person by your side.

5️⃣ Agile Business & Prototyping — How to research your problems and users, build prototypes at record speed, test them against the market, and iterate with the latest Tech & AI.


Format:
- 4 virtual, live sessions of 2 hours each.
- Taught by experts in each topic (To Be Announced / who's interested?) + top practitioner mentors supporting (still looking!).
- Networking sessions per program + one session at the end for all programs together (+ current Ignia community)
- The Ignia Promise — everyone walks away with usable deliverables. Sales folks with a working funnel, PMs with rituals, tools, and prompts tailored to their projects, Devs with real builds.

What do you think? What other topic sounds interesting? Who's down to be an instructor/mentor?
