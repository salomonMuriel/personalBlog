How to build a company (with [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/)'s real timeline) 👀

The real Ignia story: I started researching December 15, met 107 people through April 20, officially partnered with [Camilo Bonilla](https://linkedin.com/in/camilobonilla) on April 26, built the product in ~1 month, launched to market on June 14, first sale in 8 days. 58 days later we're at ~30 enrolled students and a B2B deal. 140M+ COP in sales in under 2 months, with zero outside investment.

What do you think? Unrealistic? I actually think Camilo and I took too long 🙈 — in 3 months we could easily have done the same.
