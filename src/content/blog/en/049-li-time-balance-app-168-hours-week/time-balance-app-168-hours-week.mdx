I built an app to achieve balance while staying mega productive ⬇️ Free!

Today I had lunch with a group of entrepreneurial women with fire 🔥 — the Laura duo from [Provi](https://www.linkedin.com/company/provi-investments/) ([Laura Cadena Sanchez](https://linkedin.com/in/laura-cadena-sanchez-84571682) + [Laura Camila Mejia Vargas](https://linkedin.com/in/laura-camila-mejia-vargas)), [Daniela Rojas](https://linkedin.com/in/daniela-rojas-osorio) from [Teia Invest](https://www.linkedin.com/company/teia-invest/), and [Carolina Franco 🦋🌊](https://linkedin.com/in/francocarolina) who's dreaming up a project in Stealth Mode.

We talked about balance (whether you're building something or not!) How often do we actually take time for ourselves? How much of "our time" do we waste scrolling 📱? Are we actually doing the things that move us?

A simple exercise is to map out your day and week in time blocks — what you're spending time on today vs. what you'd want to spend it on tomorrow. There are only 168 hours in a week!

Once you fill in the "today" map, all you have to do is start choosing what actually adds value and what you want to do tomorrow.

I'm doing mentoring sessions with a couple of people where we've already applied this, and the change that comes from facing that conscious choice is incredible. Making room for hobbies (like embroidery, video games, cooking) instead of losing it to doomscrolling ☠️
