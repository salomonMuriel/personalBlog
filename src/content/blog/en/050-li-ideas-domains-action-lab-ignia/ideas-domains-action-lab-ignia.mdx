Who else constantly comes up with business ideas but never launches them? 🙋‍♂️
Related vice: buying web domains to use "someday."

Just a few examples:
➡️ [membranza.com](http://membranza.com) - for the family history app
➡️ [repitis.com](http://repitis.com) - latest idea: STEM subscription box for kids)
➡️ [ignite.lat](http://ignite.lat) - previous name for ignia
➡️ [impresionante.co](http://impresionante.co) - for importing and selling 3D printers
➡️ [bigassants.com](http://bigassants.com) - for an idea to export big-butt ants 15 years ago, until I found out they were endangered

I've got a whole bunch more ideas saved on my site. From all of those on that list — I launched zero!

Ignia was one of many, but the one where I finally took the leap 🚀. And that's the whole difference: taking the first step.
