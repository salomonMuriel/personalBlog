I used to think... and now I think 🤔

I used to think that winning awards and getting press coverage meant you were a successful entrepreneur. Now I think that revenue and margin is what points toward success.

I used to think that what mattered was just getting things done, by any means necessary. Now I think people come first, and results follow.

I used to think that without investment you couldn't do anything. Now I think everything can be reduced to a bootstrappable version.

I used to think I could wake up at 10AM, wrap up at 4PM and everything would still be fine. Now I know that if you don't give it everything, there's no point doing it at all.

Thanks [Pedro Mejia](https://linkedin.com/in/pedromejiar) for the inspiration. What did you used to think that you no longer do?
