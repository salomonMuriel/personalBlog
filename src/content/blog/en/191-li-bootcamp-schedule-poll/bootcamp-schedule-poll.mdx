Hey network! Open question.

We're about to launch our Bootcamps — programs focused on specific skills. We'll have tracks in Retail, Branding, Tech for Devs and Non-Devs, and more 👀

They'll be in-person, in Bogotá — and here's the question. We want to figure out the best time slot.

All of them are ~3 to 4 hours of session + a breakfast/lunch or dinner after for networking (included in the bootcamp, obviously!)

Which of these schedules sounds best, thinking these would be on weekdays?

**Poll results:**
- 7am - 11am (+ brunch): 31 votes
- 8:30am - 12:30pm (+ lunch): 23 votes
- 4:30pm - 8:30pm (+ dinner): 81 votes ✅ Winner
