> *Originally posted on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/smuriel_ecosistema-emprendedor-hace-llamado-urgente-activity-7347010909266960385-sfe4)*

In 2017, [iNNpulsa Colombia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/innpulsa-colombia/) (through [Apps.co](http://Apps.co)) accelerated my first Startup: [PrestaGente](https://www.linkedin.com/company/prestagente/).

I had incredible mentors ([Alejandra López Álvarez](https://linkedin.com/in/alejandralopezalvarez), [Ricardo Calderón Magaña](https://linkedin.com/in/ricardo-calderon-magana), [Pedro Villegas](https://linkedin.com/in/pedro-villegas), [Camilo Jiménez Fuentes](https://linkedin.com/in/camilohjimenez)), I made my first connections in large-scale entrepreneurship, shared spaces with others who were just starting out and are now big names ([Naranja Media](https://www.linkedin.com/company/naranja-media/), [We Are Content](https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-growth-field/), [Symplifica](https://www.linkedin.com/company/symplifica/)), and met people who supported every step of the way and whom I still run into today ([Tatiana León](https://linkedin.com/in/tatiana-león-090ba13), [Alejandro Vizcaino Matallana](https://linkedin.com/in/alejandro-vizcaino-matallana-63bab243), [Daniel Martin](https://linkedin.com/in/daniel-martin-prieto)).

From all those connections came the eventual sale of PrestaGente, which led to creating (and shutting down) [Beriblock](https://www.linkedin.com/company/beriblock/), and the know-how to create and sell Finco (now [RED Atlas](https://www.linkedin.com/company/redatlas/)).

Innpulsa was the first step on my path, just as it was for many other entrepreneurs in Colombia.

Then they managed Data Science for All by [Correlation One](https://www.linkedin.com/company/correlation-one/), the program through which I "consecrated" myself as a Data Scientist, gained the know-how to take Finco forward, and helped build a Data community in the country. From there I met incredibly talented Data professionals like [Rafael Sanabria](https://linkedin.com/in/rfsan), [Wilmar Sepulveda Herrera](https://linkedin.com/in/wilmar3752), [Rasheed Sabar](https://linkedin.com/in/rasheedsabar), [Ana Maria Lopez Moreno](https://linkedin.com/in/amlopez81), and many more.

Let's build and strengthen the institutions that fuel innovation and entrepreneurship in this country.
