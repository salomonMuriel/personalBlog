🎁 I'm giving away weekly mentorship spots to 3 people with fire 🔥

I know how hard it is to build something from scratch. How do you start? What if it goes badly? How do you take the first step (or the second, or the third)?

Building something isn't just technical: how do I balance it with my life and interests? If I've already started, suddenly my work/life balance goes out the window and I burn out. How do I keep going?

3 years ago I gave my first real mentorship session with [Nicolás Varón](https://linkedin.com/in/nicolasvaronrodriguez) and I loved the experience. We're good friends today. Recently I started with [Maria Fernanda Montejo Quiceno](https://linkedin.com/in/mariafernandamontejo) and the change in just 4 sessions has been incredible.

So I want to meet new mentees. Zero strings attached. I charge NOTHING — not for the first session, not for any of them (message Nico or Mafe if you want to ask them). Requirements: Have fire 🔥 to build something new, take an existing project to the next level, or reinvent yourself professionally. Doesn't matter the industry, your expertise, your age, or your background.

To apply, just two steps:
1️⃣ Comment "FIRE" on this post
2️⃣ Sign up and attend an Ignia Action Lab info session at [https://www.ignia.lat](https://www.ignia.lat)

I'll choose the new mentees next Wednesday from those who attended the session between July 30th and August 5th.

If you know someone with a TON of hustle 🚀 who needs that support to break out of inertia and launch something new or grow their current project, tag them here or share this opportunity with them.
I want to meet them.
