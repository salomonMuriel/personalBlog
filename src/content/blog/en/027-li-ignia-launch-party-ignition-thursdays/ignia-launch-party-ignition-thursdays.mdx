Two quick announcements 💣

1. What a Saturday! 🔥 We officially launched [Ignia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/igniaeducation/) and the energy at El Irish Pub 🍀 ([The Pub SAS](https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-pub-sas/)) was spectacular. We shared everything about the Action Lab (details 👉 [www.ignia.lat/action-lab](http://www.ignia.lat/action-lab)), there was totally organic networking with beers 🍻, incredible vibe. Thank you [Andrés](https://linkedin.com/in/andresfmendez), [Francisco](https://linkedin.com/in/francisco-mendoza-5a3545158), and all 50+ attendees for coming and sharing the moment.

2. To keep the flame burning, "Ignition Thursdays" are born. A free, open weekly coworking space for anyone who wants to come, connect, and build in community. We kick off this Thursday, June 19, at the Irish Pub in Quinta Camacho starting at 2:00 PM. See you there!
