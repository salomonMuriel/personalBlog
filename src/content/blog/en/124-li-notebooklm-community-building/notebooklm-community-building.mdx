Yesterday at our Thursday Coworking, [Emma Barrientos Arismendi](https://linkedin.com/in/emma-barrientos-arismendi) was asking me — how do you build a community?

With this NotebookLM I've studied tons of resources on the topic. My top 3 things to keep in mind:

1. Clear Purpose — The community needs a clear idea of what unites it. For Ignia, it's bringing together people with fire 🔥
2. Consistency — Consistency is everything. Whether weekly, monthly, whatever — it should NEVER get cancelled.
3. Be Active — You can't be a laid-back host. You have to welcome people, keep conversations going, structure things, and create engagement that way.
