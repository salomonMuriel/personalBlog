Co-building in community — my motto for the decade. You get more done collaborating than stepping over each other.

As part of that, I set out to connect as much as possible with Colombia's Education ecosystem. Last year I talked to 150+ people (107 between January and April alone) from this world to understand what's broken and what can be fixed.

I found [Colombia Edtech](https://www.linkedin.com/company/colombiaedtech1/) — and decided to collaborate as much as possible in making it a world-class association, so we can advance education in Colombia together with other entrepreneurs in the sector.

🚀 Today I'm thrilled to announce that I was officially elected as a member of the Colombia Edtech board 🚀

It's exciting to dream of co-building with all the members, the rest of the board, and my great friend [Andrés Méndez](https://linkedin.com/in/andresfmendez) — with people who have fire in their hearts to make the world and education a little better every day.
